Dear Prof. Kamath, 
 
<p> 
<ICE-IND:W1B-026#131:1>
I am pleased to inform you that Indian
Association for American  
Studies is planning to organise a 2-day Seminar in Calcutta at
Lincoln Centre  
on September 30 and October 1. </p> 
 
<p> 
<ICE-IND:W1B-026#132:1>
The theme we have chosen for the seminar is
<quote> "  
Interactions: India and the United States" </quote> . 

<ICE-IND:W1B-026#133:1>
Under this broad theme we plan to have four different
sessions, each  
dealing with political, historical, socio-economic and
cultural. 

<ICE-IND:W1B-026#134:1>
Since American Studies is interdisciplinary it is
likely that there  
might be some overlapping between sessions, but we would like
the papers  
presented at the Seminar to belong to the particular session
in which they  
are taken up. 

<ICE-IND:W1B-026#135:1>
We would also like to focus our papers on the recent
past including  
Indo-U.S. interactions resulting from economic
liberalisation and the  
signing of GATT. </p> 
 
<p> 
<ICE-IND:W1B-026#136:1>
I shall be much obliged if you could kindly agree
to come to  
Calcutta and present your paper on the topic. 

<ICE-IND:W1B-026#137:1>
I hope you will let me know the title of your paper
along with a  
brief abstract in good time to include it in the Seminar. </p>

 
<p> 
<ICE-IND:W1B-026#138:1>
We hope to be able to provide the travel costs as
well as local  
hospitality for two days. </p> 
 
<p> 
<ICE-IND:W1B-026#139:1>
Looking forward to hearing from you and with kind
regards, </p>
