<h> <it> 5. INTER-LIBRARY LOAN SERVICE : </it> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#50:1>
Books required for Post-Graduate teaching and research will be
lent to libraries of such constituent and affiliated colleges and
Institutions as are recognized for Post-Graduate teaching and research,
and to the libraries of other Universities, subject to the availability of
books. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#51:1>
2. Any publication marked as Reference Work, or as valuable or
rare, or any book that is in constant demand will not be issued. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#52:1>
3. The University Library will bear the expenses, if any, for
forwarding the books to the borrowing library.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#53:1>
The borrowing however will have to make its own arrangements to
return the books within the specified period.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#54:1>
When books are returned by <O> <&> page </&> 4 </O> post or rail the
charges will be defrayed by the borrowing library.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#55:1>
The borrowing library will have to make full compensation if a
book is lost or damaged. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#56:1>
4. Not more than FIFTEEN books will be lent to any library & the
period of such loan will not ordinarily exceed 15 clear days. </p>

<h> <it> 6. ISSUE SYSTEM : </it> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#58:1>
The student-readers will get at the time of membership as many
tickets as the number of books they are eligible to borrow.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#59:1>
On each occasion a book is borrowed a <w> Reader's </w> Ticket is
to be given at the Issue Counter & the Identity Card shown.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#60:1>
The ticket will be returned to the Reader only when the book is
returned. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#61:1>
<w> Reader's </w> Tickets will be issued to eligible persons
by the Librarian on an application to be made in the prescribed form.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#62:1>
Such tickets shall not be transferable, shall be valid for one
academic year at a time, and shall be cancelled by the Chairman of the
Library Committee on the recommendation of the Librarian for infringement
of any of the Library rules or for any other misconduct on the part of the
holder of such a ticket. </p>

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#63:1>
<h> <bold> a) Loss of <w> Reader's </w> Tickets : </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#64:1>
In case of loss of a student <w> borrower's </w> Ticket, a
duplicate ticket will be issued to the borrower on application to the
University Librarian mentioning the circumstances in which it was lost.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#65:1>
The charge for such a duplicate <w> borrower's </w> ticket will be
Rs. 2/-. </p>

<h> <it> 7. GENERAL RULES : </it> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#67:1>
i) Before entering the Library readers shall sign the <quote> "
Gate Register" </quote> and show the Identity Card / Membership Card at the
Reception Counter.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#68:1>
They shall also deposit their private books and personal belongings
such as an umbrella, bag, file etc., at the reception counter. </p> <O>
<&> page </&> 5 </O>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#69:1>
ii) No books shall be removed from the Library by any person
until he passes a signed receipt to the Librarian for the books he desires to
get issued. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#70:1>
iii) No publication which is a part of the Reference Section
of the Library or specially labelled as valuable for its rarity or for its
illustrations shall be issued out of the Library except with the special
permission previously obtained from the Librarian. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#71:1>
iv) Borrowers shall not sub-lend books belonging to the
Library to any other person. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#72:1>
v) Books & Periodicals which have been issued out are liable to
be recalled at any time.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#73:1>
When so recalled they must be returned to the Library within the
specified time. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#74:1>
vi) Readers shall not write upon, damage, <}> <-> turndown
</-> <+> teardown </+> </}> the leaves of, or make any mark upon any book,
manuscript or map belonging to the Library; nor shall they lay the paper
on which they are writing upon any book, manuscript, or map. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#75:1>
vii) Erasure of any mark or stamp, <}> <-> lable </-> <+> label
</+> </}> etc., on a book, manuscript, or map belonging to the library will
be considered as misconduct. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#76:1>
viii) Any reader observing a defect in, or damage to or any
writing upon any book or manuscript shall point out the same to the Librarian
immediately. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#77:1>
ix) an overdue charge of 10 np. per volume per day wil be levied
if a book is kept beyond the permissible period of loan. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#78:1>
x) A book may be renewed for a further period if the same is not
in demand. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#79:1>
xi) A borrower shall be responsible for the safe custody and
return of a publication issued to him.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#80:1>
In the event of its being lost or damaged he shall pay the entire
cost of replacement. </p> <O> <&> page </&> 6 </O>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#81:1>
xii) Losses of books borrowed should be immediately reported
in writing to the Librarian.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#82:1>
Replacement costs must be paid immediately.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#83:1>
Overdue charges will be collected from the borrower until the
replacement cost is paid. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#84:1>
xiii) Students and Research workers shall have to obtain a
<quote> " Clearance Certificate" </quote> from the Librarian when they cease
to be University Students.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#85:1>
On the production of such a certificate only they shall claim their
deposit money from the University. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-008#86:1>
xiv) A borrrower shall withdraw his/her amount of deposit within
three months after the declaration of Results.

<ICE-IND:W2D-008#87:1>
If not withdrawn within the prescribed time it may be forfeited.
</p>
