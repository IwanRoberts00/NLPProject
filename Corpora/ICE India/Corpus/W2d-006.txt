<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-006#4:1>
The M.A. Course in entire English will consist of eight Papers of
which five will be core Papers and the remaining three electives.

<ICE-IND:W2D-006#5:1>
There would be five groups of electives subdivided into 2 subgroups
( A) of two papers and ( B) of one paper.

<ICE-IND:W2D-006#6:1>
Of these five groups, a student may select any one.

<ICE-IND:W2D-006#7:1>
From the group so selected the student may choose either all the three
Papers or Paper-IV & VII from sub-group A, and any one elective as Paper-VIII
from sub-group B. </p>

<p> 
<ICE-IND:W2D-006#8:1>
The objective of the core literature Papers is to provide the
student with an adequate knowledge of the tradition of English Literature and
respond independently to literary works; and the objective of the core
language paper is to provide for an advanced study of the structure and
function of Modern English with a view to sharpening the <w> student's </w>
language sense. </p>
