<ICE-IND:W1B-013#143:1>
Craft means an Art, trade or skill. 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#144:1>
Each one is a craftsman of his / her Vocation. 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#145:1>
One should share his vocational talents with others
especially with the upcoming younger generation. 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#146:1>
It is expected that every one of us should adopt and
practice  
<quote> ` THE FOUR WAY TEST' </quote> . </p> 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#147:1>
1) Is it truth ? 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#148:1>
2) Is it fair to all concerned ? 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#149:1>
3) Will it be beneficial to all concerned ? 

<ICE-IND:W1B-013#150:1>
4) Will it build goodwill & better friendship ?
