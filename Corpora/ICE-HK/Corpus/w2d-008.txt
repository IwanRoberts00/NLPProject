<ICE-HK:W2D-008#1:1>
<bold> HOW TO APPLY FOR REGISTERING AS AN ELECTRICAL WORKER </bold>
</h> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#2:1>
<bold> Purpose of Registration </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#3:1>
With the enactment of the Electricity Ordinance in 1990 all
electrical workers engaged in electrical work on fixed electrical
installations must be registered with the Electrical &ampersand; Mechanical
Services Department &obrack; EMSD &cbrack; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#4:1>
The purpose is to ensure that such work is carried out only by
qualified electrical workers in order to improve electrical safety . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#5:1>
<bold> Who Should Be Registered </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#6:1>
Any person who wishes to carry out electrical work should be
properly registered with EMSD .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#7:1>
Electrical work means work in relation to the installation ,
commissioning , inspection , testing , maintenance , modification or repair
of a low voltage or high voltage fixed electrical installation and includes
the supervision and certification of the work and design of the installation .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#8:1>
Examples of fixed electrical installations are distribution boards ,
wiring installations , lighting fittings , etc. , that are fixed in buildings
and premises . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#9:1>
Persons who are engaged in work on electrical installations other
than fixed electrical installations need not be registered .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#10:1>
Examples of such electrical installations are portable electrical
home appliances such as table lamps , television sets , refrigerators , etc .
</p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#11:1>
<bold> Registration Requirements </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#12:1>
There are five grades of certificate of registration .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#13:1>
Each applicant can only apply for one grade .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#14:1>
To be qualified , an applicant must possess the qualifications ,
training and experience appropriate to the grade that he is applying for .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#15:1>
Registration requirements of the various grades are detailed in the
Appendix . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#16:1>
<bold> How to Apply </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#17:1>
Applications for registration as electrical workers can be made
to the EMSD .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#18:1>
The procedures for new application are as follows :

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#19:1>
&obrack; 1 &cbrack; Obtain an application form &obrack; Form 8 &dash;
New/Renewal/Change of Grade of Electrical Work Application for Electrical
Worker Registration &cbrack; from District Offices , or at the Customer
Service Office , EMSD , Ground Floor , 98 Caroline Hill Road , Causeway Bay ,
Hong Kong .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#20:1>
&obrack; 2 &cbrack; Complete the application form carefully according
to the instruction sheets attached to it .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#21:1>
&obrack; 3 &cbrack; Submit the completed application form together
with an application fee and copies of supporting documents in person to the
Customer Service Office , EMSD . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#22:1>
For renewal of registration , the applicant should also use Form
8 and submit the application with application fee and copies of supporting
documents in person or by mail to the Customer Service Office , EMSD .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#23:1>
Renewal of registration should be made at least one month before and
no earlier than four months before the expiry date . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#24:1>
Application fees are not refundable .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#25:1>
A certificate of registration for an electrical worker is valid for
three years . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#26:1>
<bold> Enquiries </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#27:1>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#53:1>
<bold> Grade C Electrical Work </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#54:1>
A Grade C applicant must have the qualifications and experience

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#57:1>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#58:1>
&obrack; b &cbrack; has held , or qualified to hold , a Grade B

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#61:1>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#62:1>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#63:1>
A Grade R applicant must have special training and have at least

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#64:1>

equipment and installations .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#67:1>

voltage electrical equipment and installations . </p> <h>

<bold> HOW TO APPLY REGISTERING AS AN ELECTRICAL CONTRACTOR </bold>
</h> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#80:2>
<bold> Purpose of Registration </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#81:2>
With the enactment of the Electricity Ordinance in 1990 all
electrical contractors engaged in electrical work on fixed electrical
installations must be registered with the Electrical &ampersand; Mechanical
Services Department &obrack; EMSD &cbrack; . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#82:2>
The purpose of registration is to ensure that such work is
carried out only by qualified electrical workers through qualified electrical
contractors to improve electrical safety . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#83:2>
<bold> Who Should Be Registered </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#84:2>
Any person who wishes to do business as an electrical contractor
or contract to carry out electrical work should apply for registration as an
electrical contractor . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#85:2>
Electrical work means work in relation to the installation ,
commissioning , inspection , testing , maintenance , modification or repair
of a low voltage or high voltage fixed electrical installation and includes
the supervision and certification of the work and design of the installation .
</p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#86:2>
Examples of fixed electrical installations are distribution
boards , wiring installations , lighting fittings , etc. , which are fixed in
buildings and premises . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#87:2>
Companies who are engaged in work on electrical installations
other than fixed electrical installations need not be registered .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#88:2>
Examples of such electrical installations are portable electrical
home appliances , like table lamps , television sets , refrigerators , etc. ,
which are connected to electricity supplies by means of plug and socket
arrangements . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#89:2>
<bold> Registration Requirements </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#90:2>
To be qualified as a registered electrical contractor , the
applicant must either employ at least one registered electrical worker or
&obrack; a &cbrack; if the applicant is an individual , he must be a
registered electrical worker; or &obrack; b &cbrack; if the applicant is a
partnership , one of the partners must be a registered electrical worker .
</p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#91:2>
<bold> How to Apply </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#92:2>
Applications for registration as electrical contractors can be
made to the EMSD .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#93:2>
The procedures for applications are as follows :

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#94:2>
&obrack; 1 &cbrack; Obtain an application form &obrack; Form 1 &dash;
New/Renewal Application for Registration as an Electrical Contractor &cbrack;
from District Offices , or at the Customer Service Office , Electrical
&ampersand; Mechanical Services Department , Ground Floor , 98 Caroline Hill
Road , Causeway Bay , Hong Kong .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#95:2>
&obrack; 2 &cbrack; Complete the application form carefully according
to the instruction sheets attached to it .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#96:2>
&obrack; 3 &cbrack; Submit the completed application form together
with supporting documents and an application fee in person to the Customer
Service Office , EMSD . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-008#97:2>
For renewal of registration , the applicant should also use Form
1 and submit the application with supporting documents and an application fee .
&obrack; same application fee as for new registration &cbrack; to the
Customer Service Office , EMSD .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#98:2>
Renewal of registration should be made at least one month before and
no earlier than four months , before the expiry date .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#99:2>
Application fees are not refundable .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#100:2>
A certificate of registration for an electrical contractor is valid
for three years . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#116:2>
<bold> General Duties of a Registered Electrical Contractor </bold>
</h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#117:2>
&obrack; 1 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor shall not
employ a person other than a registered electrical worker to do electrical
work except as provided in Section 32 of the Electricity Ordinance .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#118:2>
&obrack; 2 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor shall ensure
that no registered electrical worker employed by him shall do electrical work
that the worker is not entitled to do under the Electricity Ordinance .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#119:2>
&obrack; 3 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor shall not
cause or knowingly allow : &dash; &obrack; a &cbrack; a registered electrical
worker employed by him; or &obrack; b &cbrack; a person employed by him in
the circumstances specified under Section 32 of the Electricity Ordinance ,
to do electrical work in contravention of the Electricity Ordinance .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#120:2>
&obrack; 4 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor shall
effectively supervise the registered electrical workers employed by him .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#121:2>
&obrack; 5 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor , when
required by notice of the Director of Electrical &ampersand; Mechanical
Services , shall produce for inspection any record , plan or document in his
possession or under his control that the Director considers relevant for the
purpose of enforcing the Electricity Ordinance .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#122:2>
&obrack; 6 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor shall display
in a prominent place at his principal place of business the original of his
certificate of registration and a copy of his certificate of registration
issued by the Director of Electrical &ampersand; Mechanical Services at each
of his other places of business .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#123:2>
&obrack; 7 &cbrack; A registered electrical contractor shall carry
on his business as an electrical contractor only at and from each business
address registered with the Director of Electrical &ampersand; Mechanical
Services .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#124:2>
&obrack; 8 &cbrack; Where a registered electrical contractor ceases
to carry on business or where any of the following particulars change after
the registration of an electrical contractor , the contractor shall notify
the Director of Electrical &ampersand; Mechanical Services within two weeks :
&dash;

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#125:2>
&obrack; a &cbrack; the persons who are authorised to make an
endorsement &semi;

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#126:2>
&obrack; b &cbrack; a business address &semi;

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#127:2>
&obrack; c &cbrack; the name under which the electrical contractor
does business &semi;

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#128:2>
&obrack; d &cbrack; the partners in the case of a contractor
registered as a partnership &dash; and

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#129:2>
&obrack; e &cbrack; any other particulars as may be prescribed .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#130:2>
&obrack; 9 &cbrack; Where the Electricity Ordinance requires or
authorises a registered electrical worker to make a report or to certify
something , the registered electrical contractor for whom the electrical work
is done shall endorse the report or certificate .

<ICE-HK:W2D-008#131:2>
&obrack; 10 &cbrack; Where the registered electrical contractor is
the same person as the registered electrical worker who made the report or
certified something , he shall nevertheless endorse the report or certificate
in his capacity as the contractor . </I>
