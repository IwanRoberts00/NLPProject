<I> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#1:1>
<bold> HOW TO APPLY FOR A DRIVING LICENCE </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#2:1>
If you wish to drive a private car , a goods vehicle , a special
purpose vehicle , a motor cycle , a light bus , a taxi , a bus , an
articulated vehicle or an invalid carriage in Hong Kong , you need a valid
driving licence . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#3:1>
<bold> Learner  ' s Driving Licence </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#4:1>
A learner  ' s driving licence is required before you can learn to
drive any motor vehicle .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#5:1>
Application for the licence should be made on the prescribed form and
, apart from that related to commercial vehicles , submitted to the Hong Kong
Licensing Office , Kowloon Licensing Office , Kwun Tong Licensing Office or
Sha Tin Licensing Office of the Transport Department .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#6:1>
Regarding commercial vehicles , application is processed by Hong Kong
Licensing Office or Kowloon Licensing Office only , or you may send it by
registered post to Sha Tin Licensing Office . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#7:1>
If you apply for a learner  ' s licence to drive private cars ,
light goods vehicles or motor cycles , you must be at least 18 years old . If
you wish to learn to drive medium or heavy goods vehicles , special purpose
vehicles , taxis , private and public light buses , private and public buses
, you must be at least 21 years old , and have held a full driving licence
for private cars for at least three years .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#8:1>
If you apply for a learner  ' s licence for articulated vehicles , you
must be a holder of a valid full driving licence to drive medium or heavy
goods vehicles . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#9:1>
If you apply for First Issue of learner  ' s driving licence for
driving motor cycle , you are required to produce Part B &obrack; competence
test &cbrack; Pass Certificate .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#10:1>
If you apply for Renewal of learner  ' s driving licence for driving
motor cycle , you are required to produce Part C &obrack; road test &cbrack;
appointment letter . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#11:1>
When submitting your application , you must also produce your
Hong Kong Identity Card or , if you have not yet registered with the
Registration of Persons Office of the Immigration Department , an acceptable
alternative document , e.g. a passport issued outside Hong Kong . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#12:1>
On payment of the prescribed fee , a learner  ' s driving licence ,
valid for 12 months , will be issued provided you meet all the licensing
requirements . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#13:1>
<bold> Driving Test </bold> </h> If you wish to take a driving test ,
you must apply for a test form .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#14:1>
Upon application , you must produce a learner  ' s driving licence and
pay the prescribed fee .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#15:1>
A test form is valid for 18 months and entitles you to take all parts
of a test within that period .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#16:1>
It ceases to be effective if you fail in any part of the test; a new
test form is required for a further test .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#17:1>
It also ceases to be effective if you fail to attend a driving test
unless: <it> &obrack; a &cbrack; </it> you have given seven clear days  ' 
notice to the Commissioner for Transport of your inability to attend the
test; or <it> &obrack; b &cbrack; </it> the Commissioner for Transport is
satisfied that you , through circumstances beyond your control , are unable
to attend the test . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#18:1>
If you are unable to attend a driving test in the above
circumstances , a new appointment will be arranged .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#19:1>
Provided that the new appointment is arranged within 18 months from
the date of issue , the same form can be used for the test , otherwise you
have to apply for a new one . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#20:1>
For private car , light goods vehicle and motor cycle driving
tests , if you wish to be tested at centres located on Hong Kong Island ,
please apply to the Hong Kong Licensing Office .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#21:1>
For those who wish to be tested in Kowloon or New Territories ,
please apply to the Kowloon Licensing Office . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#22:1>
For all commercial vehicle driving tests , after you have
completed the application forms , forward them together with a certified
photocopy of your Hong Kong driving licence and a crossed cheque for the
correct amount of fees payable to &lsquo; the Government of the Hong Kong
Special Administrative Region &rsquo; by registered post , to Transport
Department Sha Tin Licensing Office , Sha Tin P.O. Box 55 to make an
appointment . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#23:1>
<bold> The Test </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#24:1>
There are different types of driving tests for various classes of
vehicles . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#25:1>
The driving test for a private car consists of three parts .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#26:1>
Part A is a written test of multiple choice type , consisting of
bilingual questions on the Road Users  '  Code .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#27:1>
Part B is a test of driving technique , conducted on-street or
off-street and can be combined with Part C which is a test on a road .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#28:1>
If you hold a motor cycle driving licence , you are not required to
take the written test . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#29:1>
If you apply for a driving licence to drive light goods vehicles
, you are required to take a written test on the Road Users  '  Code and a road
test .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#30:1>
However , if you hold a private car or motor cycle driving licence ,
you are not required to take the written test . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#31:1>
If you wish to take a motor cycle driving test , you are required
to enrol with a designated driving school for a mandatory training course to
acquire the basic knowledge and skills to manoeuvre a motor cycle .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#32:1>
After passing the written test and the Part B test &obrack; i.e.
Competence Test &cbrack; , you can apply for a motor cycle learner driving
licence and take part in the Part C test &obrack; i.e. Road Test &cbrack; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#33:1>
If you are the holder of a private car driving licence , you are not
required to take the written test . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#34:1>
If you apply for a taxi driving licence , you are required to
take a written test on the Road Users  '  Code , local knowledge of Hong Kong
and general legal requirements relating to taxis; neither a learner  ' s
driving licence nor a road test is required . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#35:1>
For other classes of vehicles , only a road test is required if
you are a licensed driver of private cars with the prescribed experience .
</p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#36:1>
If you enrol with a designated driving school established in
accordance with Road Traffic Ordinance , Cap. 374 , you are required to
comply with the school regulations .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#37:1>
Before you can take the driving test , you have to attend a full set
of lessons which include simulator training , lectures and both off-street
and on-road practical lessons .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#38:1>
In the event that you fail the test , you are required to attend a
repeater course before a re-test can be arranged . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#39:1>
<bold> First Issue of Full Driving Licence </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#40:1>
Application for the issue of a full driving licence &obrack;
other than full driving licence to drive motor cycles &cbrack; may be made on
the prescribed form within three years from the date of passing the driving
test .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#41:1>
The application must be accompanied by your Hong Kong Identity Card
&obrack; or an acceptable alternative document &cbrack; , your learner  ' s
driving licence , duplicate of your test form and the appropriate fee . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#42:1>
You are only eligible to apply for a probationary driving licence
upon passing the motor cycle driving test .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#43:1>
The holder of a probationary driving licence shall only be eligible
to apply for a full driving licence upon satisfactory completion of the
12-month mandatory probationary driving period . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#44:1>
<bold> Validity of Full Driving Licences </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#45:1>
A full Hong Kong Driving Licence valid for 10 years will be
issued to people aged 60 or below who apply for the first issue of a full
driving licence , or apply for the renewal of their existing driving licences
which are due for renewal .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#46:1>
For those who are aged above 60 , they will be issued with full
driving licences with a validity period less than 10 years . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#47:1>
During the validity period of your driving licence , once you
become aware that you suffer from any disease or physical disability that may
affect your driving of a vehicle , you must immediately give notice of such
fact in writing to the Commissioner for Transport . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#48:1>
<bold> Renewal of Full Driving Licence </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#49:1>
A full driving licence may be renewed at any time during the four
months before expiry .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#50:1>
Application must be made on the prescribed form , accompanied by your
old licence , Hong Kong Identity Card &obrack; or an acceptable alternative
document &cbrack; and the appropriate fee . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#51:1>
Licences which have expired for a period exceeding three years
are not renewable .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#52:1>
In such circumstances , you will be required to obtain a learner  ' s
driving licence and to undergo a new driving test .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#53:1>
However , you are not required to take the written test again . </p>
<h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#54:1>
<bold> Aged 70 and Above </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#55:1>
If you are 70 years of age or above and wish to apply for issue
or renewal of a driving licence , you must produce a medical examination
report completed by a registered medical practitioner to prove that you are
medically fit to drive and control any class of motor vehicle to which your
application refers .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#56:1>
This medical examination report must be issued within four months
from the commencement date of the new licence .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#57:1>
The relevant form &lsquo; medical examination for an applicant of 70
years of age or more for a full or learner  ' s driving licence &rsquo;
&obrack; TD 256 &cbrack; can be obtained from the licensing offices of the
Transport Department , or from the Transport Department Website . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#58:1>
<bold> Disabled Drivers </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#59:1>
If you have proved your fitness to drive a private car and
produced to the Commissioner for Transport a certificate , signed by or on
behalf of the Hospital Authority , stating that you are suffering from a
permanent disease or physical disability that causes you considerable
difficulty in walking , you may apply for the exemption from payment of the
fees required for a driving test and the issue or renewal of a driving
licence for the class of private car .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#60:1>
However , you are still required to pay the levy for the Traffic
Accident Victims Assistance Scheme . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#61:1>
<bold> Professional Drivers </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#62:1>
You must be free from conviction of an offence under Section 36
&obrack; causing death by dangerous driving &cbrack; or Section 39 &obrack;
driving a motor vehicle under the influence of drink or drugs &cbrack; of the
Road Traffic Ordinance in the previous five years , before you are eligible
for the issue of a full driving licence to drive professionally a taxi , a
light bus , a bus , a goods vehicle , an articulated vehicle , or a special
purpose vehicle , etc . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#63:1>
<bold> Traffic Accident Victims Assistance Scheme </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#64:1>
On the first issue and renewal of a driving licence , appropriate
contribution has to be made to the Traffic Accident Victims Assistance Scheme
to assist accident victims and dependants . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#65:1>
<bold> Duplicate Driving Licences </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#66:1>
If your licence is damaged , defaced , destroyed or lost , you
should report to the licensing office by filling in the prescribed form ,
together with the payment of the prescribed fee , to obtain a duplicate
licence . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#67:1>
<bold> Change of Particulars in Driving Licences </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#68:1>
A driving licence holder should notify a licensing office of the
Transport Department in writing of any change in the name , address or
identity document within 72 hours after such change . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#69:1>
<bold> Cancellation of Driving Licence </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#70:1>
If after an enquiry conducted by the Commissioner for Transport ,
it is found that your driving might endanger the public owing to mental or
physical disability , your driving licence will be cancelled immediately .
</p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#71:1>
If your licence is disqualified by a court of law , you are
required to surrender the licence to the Court or Transport Department and
stop driving immediately . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#72:1>
<bold> Overseas Driving Licence </bold> </h> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#73:1>
A. Direct Issue of Hong Kong Driving Licence </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#74:1>
A Hong Kong driving licence may be issued to you without a test
if you are the holder of an overseas driving licence issued by one of the
approved countries or places listed on the Fourth Schedule of the Road
Traffic &obrack; Driving Licences &cbrack; Regulations , Cap. 374 and is
valid or has expired for not more than three years .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#75:1>
You must also comply with one of the additional conditions: <it>
&obrack; a &cbrack; </it> have resided in the overseas country or place of
issue for not less than six months during which the licence was issued
&obrack; you may prove the six-month residence with entry and departure
stamps on your passport , school transcript, employment testimonial or tax
statement in the country or place of issue &cbrack; ; or <it> &obrack; b
&cbrack; </it> have held the licence for not less than five years prior to
the application; or <it> &obrack; c &cbrack; </it> hold a passport or
equivalent document of the country or place in which the licence was issued .
</p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#76:1>
If you do not satisfy all the conditions for direct issue , you
will be required to pass a test in all parts before a Hong Kong driving
licence is issued . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#77:1>
Application is only accepted at the Hong Kong Licensing Office .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#78:1>
It must be made on the prescribed form and accompanied by originals
and photocopies of your overseas driving licence , travel documents and Hong
Kong Identity Card together with the appropriate fee .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#79:1>
An officially certified translation of an overseas driving licence
presented in connection with the application is also required if the licence
is in a language other than English or Chinese . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#80:1>
B. Temporary Driving Licence </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#81:1>
A Temporary Driving Licence may be issued on payment of the
prescribed fee if you are taking up residence in Hong Kong and you hold a
valid overseas driving licence issued by a country or place not on the
approved list provided that you satisfy one of the additional requirements
prescribed above for direct issue of Hong Kong driving licence and that you
have applied for a driving test within three months of your arrival in Hong
Kong .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#82:1>
A Temporary Driving Licence will enable you to drive on public roads
before the test , but if you fail in any part of the driving test , your
Temporary Driving Licence will be cancelled immediately . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#83:1>
C. Visitors Driving in Hong Kong </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#84:1>
If you are a visitor to Hong Kong , you can drive on the strength
of your overseas driving licence or international driving permit . A visitor
is a person who does not plan to reside in Hong Kong for more than 12 months .
</p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#85:1>
Overseas Driving </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#86:1>
If you wish to drive abroad , you may apply for an International
Driving Permit on the prescribed form .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#87:1>
The permit will be issued if you are a resident in Hong Kong and hold
a valid Hong Kong driving licence .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#88:1>
Two photographs and the prescribed fee are required . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#93:1>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#94:1>
Application by Post </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#95:1>
Applications may be submitted in person to any of the four
licensing offices of the Transport Department or by registered post to
Transport Department Kwun Tong Licensing Office , P.O. Box 69511 , Kwun Tong
Post Office , Kowloon . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#96:1>
The relevant application forms should be properly and clearly
completed , signed and forwarded together with the appropriate fee and
relevant supporting documents &obrack; please refer to notes printed at the
back of the application form &cbrack; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#97:1>
Do not send cash by post .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#98:1>
Crossed cheques should be made payable to
&lsquo; the Government of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region &rsquo; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#99:1>
Photocopies of identity card &obrack; or acceptable alternative
document &cbrack; are acceptable . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#100:1>
If your application is in order , the new licence and other
documents would be sent to you within 10 working days .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#101:1>
Enquiries on applications by post may be made by telephone on 2349
0200 . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#102:1>
Application through Drop-in Box </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-004#103:1>
Applications for first issue/renewal of learner  ' s driving
licence and first issue/renewal of full driving licence may also be submitted
through the drop-in box at the licensing offices of the Transport Department .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#104:1>
The relevant application forms should be properly and clearly
completed , signed and forwarded together with the appropriate fee and
relevant supporting documents &obrack; please refer to notes printed at the
back of the application form &cbrack; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#105:1>
Do not submit cash with drop-in applications .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#106:1>
Crossed cheques should be made payable to &lsquo; the Government of
the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region &rsquo; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#107:1>
Photocopies of identity card &obrack; or acceptable alternative
document &cbrack; are acceptable .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#108:1>
Detailed application procedures are displayed at the drop-in box .

<ICE-HK:W2D-004#109:1>
If your application is in order , the new licence and other
documents would be sent to you within 10 working days after your submission .
