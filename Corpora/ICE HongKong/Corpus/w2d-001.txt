<ICE-HK:W2D-001#1:1>
<bold> HOW TO APPLY FOR AIRCRAFT STATION LICENCE &obrack; FOR AIRCRAFT
REGISTERED IN HONG KONG ONLY &cbrack; </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#2:1>
In Hong Kong , the establishment or maintenance of any
radiocommunication apparatus on board an aircraft registered in Hong Kong is
subject to control under the Telecommunication Ordinance , Cap. 106 .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#3:1>
If the aircraft owner wishes to establish a radio station on board the
aircraft , he/she is required to take out an Aircraft Station Licence from
the Office of the Telecommunications Authority &obrack; OFTA &cbrack; . </p>
<h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#4:1>
<bold> GENERAL LICENSING CONDITIONS </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#5:1>
The Aircraft Station Licence will only be issued to those aircraft
fulfilling the following requirements :

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#6:1>
&obrack; 1 &cbrack; possessing a Certificate of Registration of
Aircraft; or &obrack; 2 &cbrack; having been confirmed by the Civil Aviation
Department , Hong Kong , that the aircraft is under registration process; and
&obrack; 3 &cbrack; possessing a Certificate of Approval of Aircraft Radio
Installation issued by Civil Aviation Department , Hong Kong , if available .
</p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#7:1>
<bold> RADIO AND ELECTRONIC NAVIGATIONAL APPARATUS INSTALLED ON BOARD
AIRCRAFT </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#8:1>
The radio and electronic navigational apparatus installed on board
the aircraft must comply with all relevant Civil Airworthiness Requirements .
</p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#42:2>
<bold> HOW TO APPLY FOR AN AMATEUR RADIO STATION LICENCE </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#43:2>
In Hong Kong , the possession or use of any radiocommunication
apparatus is subject to control under the Telecommunication Ordinance
&obrack; Cap. 106 &cbrack; .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#44:2>
If a person wishes to establish and operate an amateur radio station
, he/she is required to take out an appropriate licence and an Authority To
Operate from the Office of the Telecommunications Authority &obrack; OFTA
&cbrack; . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#45:2>
<bold> TYPES OF LICENCE </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#46:2>
There are three types of licence , namely : &obrack; 1 &cbrack;
Amateur Station Licence &obrack; Full Operation &cbrack;

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#47:2>
This type of licence is issued for the operation of the full range of
privileges available to licensed amateurs , so long if they operate within
the authorised frequency bands and under the specified conditions .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#48:2>
&obrack; 2 &cbrack; Amateur Station Licence &obrack; Restricted
Operation &cbrack;

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#49:2>
This type of licence is issued for restricted operation only .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#50:2>
The licensee is confined to transmitting on authorised frequencies
above 50.05MHz only and under the specified conditions .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#51:2>
&obrack; 3 &cbrack; Amateur Station Licence &obrack; Club Operation
&cbrack;

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#52:2>
This type of licence is issued for the operation of amateur equipment
owned by a club , or association , etc. which is formally registered in Hong
Kong and the purpose of which is for the self-training , intercommunication
and technical investigations of its members without any pecuniary interest
being incurred .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#53:2>
The radio equipment so licensed shall be operated by , or in the
presence and under the supervision of , a person who has been authorised by
the licensee for this purpose and who possesses the appropriate Authority To
Operate issued by the Office of the Telecommunications Authority . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#54:2>
The annual licence fee of an Amateur Station licence is &dollar;
150 .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#55:2>
Payment can be made by cash , crossed cheque of local bank or
bankdraft payable to &lsquo; Office of the Telecommunications Authority
&rsquo; . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#56:2>
<bold> AUTHORITY TO OPERATE &obrack; ATO &cbrack; </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#57:2>
The ATO is an identity card to identify the holder of the card as
a person who is qualified to operate licensed amateur radio equipment in Hong
Kong .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#58:2>
When an amateur operates licensed mobile and portable amateur
equipment outdoors , the ATO is the document to identify himself if
questioned by the appropriate authorities .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#59:2>
The fee of an ATO is &dollar; 160 and it is valid for five years .
</p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#60:2>
<bold> QUALIFICATIONS </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#61:2>
Applicants for an amateur station licence and/or an ATO with full
privileges must satisfy the following requirements :

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#62:2>
&obrack; 1 &cbrack; must be at least 16 years old; &obrack; 2
&cbrack; have passed the Radio Amateur Examination of the City and Guilds of
the London Institute , or Hong Kong Radio Amateurs  '  Examination conducted by
OFTA , or equivalent; and &obrack; 3 &cbrack; have passed the Morse test
conducted by the Office of the Telecommunications Authority . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#63:2>
Applicants who have not passed the Morse test may apply for an
amateur station licence and/or an ATO with restricted privileges only . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#64:2>
If the applicant is under 18 years of age , the licence will be
issued in the name of his or her parent or guardian , who will be the person
responsible for the observation of the licence conditions . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#78:2>
<bold> FOREIGN VISITORS </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#79:2>
Amateur radio operator  ' s licences issued in certain foreign
countries are recognised in Hong Kong .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#80:2>
Foreign visitors holding a valid licence issued by the appropriate
authority in any of these countries will be considered acceptable . Any
visitor who wishes to operate licensed amateur radio equipment in Hong Kong
must apply for an ATO and , if he/she also wishes to possess and establish
his/her own station in Hong Kong , shall apply for an amateur station licence .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#81:2>
A visitor will be normally issued an ATO and where appropriate , an
amateur station licence with his/her home call sign with the prefix of VR2 .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#82:2>
A visitor who has already applied for Hong Kong Identity card will be
issued an ATO and , where appropriate , an amateur station licence with a
local call sign in the VR2 series .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#83:2>
The ATO and the licence may be renewed depending on his/her further
stay in Hong Kong .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#84:2>
For further details or a list of the countries recognised , please
contact the Maritime Services Sub-section , Office of the Telecommunications
Authority &obrack; Tel . 2961 6608 &cbrack; . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#121:3>
<bold> HOW TO APPLY FOR HOTEL TELEVISION SERVICES LICENCE </bold>
</h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#122:3>
In accordance with the Telecommunication Ordinance , the
provision and operation of closed circuit television entertainment and
information services within a hotel is subject to license under the Hotel
Television Services &obrack; HTS &cbrack; Licence . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#123:3>
Under the terms of the HTS Licence , paid advertising and
pay-per-view are permitted .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#124:3>
There is no restriction on advertising time in hotel television
broadcasts , but the advertising will have to conform to standards laid down
in the Code of Practice relating to Advertising Standards issued under the
Television Ordinance .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#125:3>
The Commissioner for Television and Entertainment Licensing will
also monitor the programme materials broadcast within a hotel that these
programme materials do not breach the Control of Obscene and Indecent
Articles Ordinance . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#126:3>
Application for HTS Licence should be made in writing to: Office
of the Telecommunications Authority , 36/F , Wu Chung House , 213 Queen  ' s
Road East , Wan Chai , Hong Kong . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#127:3>
Only hotels licensed by the Licensing Authority under the Hotel
and Guesthouse Accommodation Ordinance will be considered by the
Telecommunications Authority for the issue of HTS Licence . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#128:3>
<bold> INFORMATION REQUIRED FROM APPLICANT </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#129:3>
An applicant should provide the following information:

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#130:3>
&obrack; a &cbrack; A copy of the Business Registration Certificate
and the Certificate of Incorporation of the hotel , and , a copy of the
Business Registration Certificate of the parent company , if applicable .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#131:3>
&obrack; b &cbrack; A copy of the Membership Certificate issued by
the Hong Kong Hotels Association , or , a copy of the licence issued by the
Licensing Authority under the Hotel and Guesthouse Accommodation Ordinance .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#132:3>
&obrack; c &cbrack; A description of the system to be installed
including number of channels , proposed frequency plan and other details of
the distribution system together with a schematic diagram showing the
detailed connections of the proposed system .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#133:3>
&obrack; d &cbrack; A description of the services to be provided
together with a list showing the channel numbers and programme titles .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#134:3>
&obrack; e &cbrack; The number of reception units to be connected to
the proposed system . </p> <h>

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#135:3>
<bold> LICENCE FEE </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2D-001#136:3>
The licence fees would be calculated in accordance with the
following formula:

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#137:3>
&obrack; a &cbrack; A fee of &dollar;150 for every transmitter unit
functioning as an independent source of television programmes and &dollar;80
for every reception unit functioning as an independent output from the system
payable on the grant or renewal of a HTS Licence , and , when apparatus for
radiocommunication is possessed , used , established or maintained in the
distribution system of the service licensed , the following additional fees
shall be payable on the grant and renewal of the licence:

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#138:3>
&obrack; 1 &cbrack; A fee of &dollar;75 per MHz of occupied
bandwidth for every radio transmitter in the distribution system , and

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#139:3>
&obrack; 2 &cbrack; A fee of &dollar;75 per MHz of occupied
bandwidth for every radio receiver in the distribution system .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#140:3>
&obrack; b &cbrack; For the purpose of determining the fees payable
under LICENCE FEE &obrack; a &cbrack; in respect of the transmitter units and
the reception units , the number of units shall be those functioning at the
time when the licence is granted or , as the case may be , renewed .

<ICE-HK:W2D-001#141:3>
And , for the purpose of determining the fees payable under LICENCE
FEE &obrack; a &cbrack; &obrack; 1 &cbrack; and &obrack; 2 &cbrack; , the
occupied bandwidth shall be that occupied at the time when the licence is
granted or renewed . </p> <h>
