<ICE-HK:W2A-035#2:1>
DRAINAGE AND PLUMBING REGULATIONS </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#3:1>
The installation of drainage and plumbing works is governed by the
Building &obrack;  Standards of sanitary Fitments , Plumbing , Drainage Works and
Latrines &cbrack; Regulations including items for standards of sanitary fitments ,
plumbing - soil fitments and waste fitments , plumbing - pipes and eaves
gutters , drainage works , septic tanks , cesspools , testing of drainage
works , certain work to be carried out by the Building Authority , latrines ,
etc . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#4:1>
To establish the minimum number of water closet fitments ,
lavatory basins , baths or showers required , the usable floor space &obrack;  which
means the total floor area of a building excluding public circulation area ,
building service rooms and toilets , kitchens as well as bathrooms &cbrack; is
calculated .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#5:1>
There is a provision rate of persons for different use of buildings
e.g. it is one person every 3.25 s.m . for tenement houses , it is one
person every 9 s.m . for office and industrial , it is one person every 1.55m
for restaurant .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#6:1>
The number of persons in places of public entertainment and cinemas is
based on the maximum capacity of the area .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#7:1>
The number of persons residing in residential buildings as determined
by the Building Authority .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#8:1>
With the number of male and female persons calculated , the number of
fittings can be established from specified tables in the Regulations . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#9:1>
In case of water closet fitments , the flushing cistern discharge
water in each occasions not less than 9 litre and not more than 14 litre .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#10:1>
The soil fitment is provided with a suitable trap of water seal not
less than 50mm .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#11:1>
The internal diameter of such trap is not less than 80mm but can be
modified to 50mm upon Building Authority  ' s approval . </p> <O> Two photos
</O> <&> Chinese translation </&>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#12:1>
Water closet cubicle for disabled persons is not less than 1.5m
by 7.75m in area .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#13:1>
A water closet and a wash basin suitable for use by the disabled are
installed .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#14:1>
There are also at least 3 grab bars fixed on the walls .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#15:1>
The door has 750mm clearance and opens outwards . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#16:1>
Ventilating pipes are provided for drains and sewers projecting
not less than 1m above the roof of the building where it is attached . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#17:1>
Drain pipes , anti-syphonage pipes and ventilation pipes inside a
building can be fixed inside a pipe duct which is provided with access panels
to reach to all pipe connections .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#18:1>
Falls of drains are also noted as these may cross with structures or
affecting headroom . </p> <O> A photo and a table </O>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#19:1>
Manholes are provided at change of direction of drains &obrack;  except
small changes &cbrack; and not exceeding 60m length .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#20:1>
Covers for manholes are standard cast iron double-sealed covers .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#21:1>
A second cover incorporating the adjacent floor finish can be added
for aesthetics reason . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#22:1>
Cleaning eyes are provided at bends for soil pipes and waste
pipes to facilitate cleaning .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#23:1>
Thus access panels are designed for accessibility to these cleaning
eyes e.g . at side or end wall of a bath-tub , false ceiling to reach
cleaning eyes of upper floor . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#24:1>
Cesspools , septic tanks and sewerage treatment plants are
constructed in accordance with the Regulations .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#25:1>
Testing is carried out to satisfy they Environmental Protection unit .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#26:1>
Grease traps are provided for commercial kitchens and carparks to
relieve contamination to nature . </p> <&> Chinese translation </&>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#27:1>
It is noted that connection of the drain to public sewer and
construction of the last manhole are carried out by the Drainage Division of
Highways Departments , such cost involved will be recovered from the owner of
the development . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#28:1>
An aperture for gas water heater is required to be provided at
bathrooms and shower rooms .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#29:1>
The size of the standard aperture is 380 &times; 380mm or 240 &times; 240mm when
only a shower is installed .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#30:1>
Location of the aperture is governed by regulation with unobstructed
area around .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#31:1>
If no heater is fitted during construction , the standard aperture
can be sealed by easily-removable brickwork or blockwork .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#32:1>
However , the position of which is made distinguishable . </p> <&>

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#33:1>
PLUMING PIPEWORK </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#34:1>
For city water supply system , materials for pipework include
galvanized steel , copper , unplasticised PVC .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#35:1>
Copper pipes or plastic coated copper pipes are used for hot water
system and inside the bathrooms .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#36:1>
Copper pipes are provided with expansion joints over 12m length .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#37:1>
However , care must be taken at installation to avoid any damages
which may lead to subsequent troublesome water leakage when all the finishes
are installed .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#38:1>
Ductile iron pipes are used to stand heavy loads when the system of
pipework passes through carriageway . </p> <O> A photo </O> <&> Chinese
translation </&> <O> A diagram </O>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#39:1>
For flush water supply system , ductile iron pipes are used as
delivery pipe for salt water; galvanized steel pipes are used as delivery
pipe for city water , nullah intake or well .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#40:1>
Other pipes of the distribution system are of PVC pipes which are
kept a minimum distance of 150mm from hot water pipes . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#41:1>
Pipes are run in walls , floor screeds or pipe ducts .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#42:1>
Pipes through walls and floors are first cast in PVC sleeves with 2
to 12mm clearance for expansion and movement .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#43:1>
Mastic sealant can be used to achieve watertightness .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#44:1>
If through fire rated walls and floors , galvanized mild steel pipe
sleeve with 20mm clearance is used .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#45:1>
The clearance space is then caulked with mineral wool .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#46:1>
Pipes through flat roofs are by case iron or galvanized mild steel
sleeve with 2 to 12 mm clearance projecting 150mm above roof finish .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#47:1>
Caulking is then applied by mastic sealant .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#48:1>
Pipes laid on roof are bedded on concrete blocks at approximately 1m
centers and 75mm clear of roof finishes . </p> <&> Chinese translation </&>

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#49:1>
DRAINAGE PIPEWORK </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#50:1>
Materials for drain pipes include concrete vitrified clay ware ,
cast iron with flexible joint , ductile iron unplasticised PVC .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#51:1>
PVC are used inside the building .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#52:1>
For down pipes , cast iron is commonly used .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#53:1>
Concrete and vitrified clay were can be constructed in large diameter
pipes .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#54:1>
Ductile iron is used when crossing over carriageway with heavy load .
</p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#55:1>
Laying of pipes outside the building is first by setting out with
levels .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#56:1>
Excavation is done in trenches .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#57:1>
On completion of excavation , the pipes are laid .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#58:1>
Beds and surrounds are provided to support the pipes: - &obrack;  1 &cbrack; <bold>
Natural bedding . </bold>

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#59:1>
For places like planters and lawn where light traffic is envisaged
and for short length of pipes , natural bedding by trimming earth to provide
uniform and solid bearing is acceptable . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#60:1>
&obrack;  2 &cbrack; <bold> Granular bedding . </bold>

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#61:1>
Bedding with coarse aggregate provides some support to the pipe which
is applicable on virgin land and light traffic . </p> <O> Two photos </O> <&>
Chinese translation </&> <O> 1 diagram </O>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#62:1>
&obrack;  3 &cbrack; <bold> Concrete bedding . </bold>

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#63:1>
Pipes are laid in continuous concrete bed or precast cradles and then
haunch and surround after testing .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#64:1>
In areas like newly filled land where settlement is possible , short
steel piles called monkey piles are provided for the foundation support to
give the strongest bedding for pipes . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#65:1>
After bedding is complete &obrack;  for concrete bedding , wait for 48
hours &cbrack; , the trenches are backfilled and compacted . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#66:1>
Sub-soil drain pipes can be concrete porous drain pipes ,
unglazed clay were field drain pipes , perforated vitrified clay were pipes
or perforated plastic pipes .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#67:1>
Natural bedding or granular bedding is provided . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#68:1>
Overflow pipes for roofs are constructed with 75mm PVC pipes
projecting 75mm over the side of the external wall .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#69:1>
The design of such is to avoid water coming down into the building in
case the surface channels are blocked up . </p> <O> A photo </O> <&> Chinese

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#70:1>
FIRE SERVICE INSTALLATION RELATED WORK </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#71:1>
Fire Service Inlet and Sprinkler Inlet poured in steel boxes with
label panels are located near the entrance of a building at prominent
location for ease of access in case of fire .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#72:1>
To give a better outlook , a concealed type of design is often used
for the panel doors . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#73:1>
Hose reels often concealed in recessed housing in typical floor .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#74:1>
The control and nozzle are at a position not more than 1350mm from
the finished floor .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#75:1>
Consideration has to be made to ensure that when the closet door are
open , the width of escape route is not decreased .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#76:1>
Alongside with hose reels are fire alarms and break glass call points
which can be installed as recessed fittings also .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#77:1>
The call points are not more than 1200mm above finished floor level .
</p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#78:1>
The hydrant outlet and riser are usually installed at the corner
of the staircase landing without affecting the swing of the staircase radius .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#79:1>
Outlets are at height of not less than 800mm nor more than 1200mm
above finished floor level . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#80:1>
The sprinkler installation affects the design of ceiling and
requires co-ordination with structure to allow for sleeve openings . </p> <O>
Two photos </O> <&> Chinese translation </&> <O> Four diagrams </O>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#81:1>
Sprinkler tank can be located underground or at roof depending on
the structure but these occupy a bulk of space .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#82:1>
Twin-end feed tank is smaller than single-end feed tank .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#83:1>
Fire service tank for the hydrant system is located on the roof to
provide the static pressure . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#84:1>
For large developments , a fire control room with annunciation
panel is required .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#85:1>
This is located near the entrance of a building . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#86:1>
Graphics relating to fire services is required .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#87:1>
Notice stating fire doors to be kept closed are required on both
sides of fire doors such as for smoke lobbies .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#88:1>
Exit signs with built-in lights is required to indicate the exit door
locations . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#89:1>
Some of the service rooms are classified as Special Hazard areas
requiring enclosure by non-combustible construction having an FRP of not less
than 2 hours , 4 hours adjoining staircase and any doors provided with FRP
not less than one hour .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#90:1>
These include: high voltage electrical switch gear , transformers ,
fire service pumps , air handling unit plant , air-conditioning unit , lift

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#100:1>
LIFT-RELATED WORK </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#101:1>
The design of lifts is subject to the Building &obrack;  Lifts)
Regulations and approval by Electrical and Mechanical Service Department .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#102:1>
Lift shafts and lift pits are constructed with at least 2 hours fire
rating period i.e . 125mm minimum think reinforced concrete .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#103:1>
In case where there is accessible floor space below the lift pit ,
additional structural requirement for the lift pit floor may be imposed by
the Building Authority . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#104:1>
Machine rooms have clear heights not less than 2.1 m .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#105:1>
The doors of such rooms are self-closing and outward open with a
fire rating period not less than half an hour . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#106:1>
For lifts that do not have landing doors in some floors ,
emergency access to the lift shaft are provided at intervals not greater than
11m for the sake of evacuating passengers in the lifts for emergency .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#107:1>
Such access have a minimum height of 1.8m and a minimum width of
0.5m , open outwards from the lift/shaft and with a fire rating period not
less than one hour .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#108:1>
A sign marking the rescue door is displayed . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#109:1>
Every landing door is constructed with not less than one hour
fire rating period .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#110:1>
Adjacent to the landing door is displayed signs of &ldquo; when
there is a fire , do not use the lift &rdquo; which is incorporated as part
of the lift lobby design . </p> <&> Chinese translation </&> <O> Three photos

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#111:1>
AIR-CONDITIONING RELATED WORK </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#112:1>
Central air-conditioning plants are exempted from gross floor
area calculation but they still occupy a lot of space and generate noise and
vibration , thus requiring acoustical consideration .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#113:1>
Chilled water pipes , air ducts and air handling units occupy space
within typical floors .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#114:1>
Louvres are required on exterior of building for intake and exhaust
air . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#115:1>
For window-type air-conditioning units in residential buildings
, wall boxes or platforms are constructed as amenity features to improve the
structural security of these installations .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#116:1>
Besides structural stability , noise , vibration , exhaust air or
dripping water are also considered . </p> <O> A photo </O> <&> Chinese

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#117:1>
REFUSE COLLECTION WORK </bold> </h>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#118:1>
Refuse collection is governed by the Building &obrack;  Refuse Storage
Chambers and Chutes &cbrack; Regulations . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#119:1>
The refuse storage chamber is provided for domestic building
with more than 1320 s.m . usable floor area and for non-domestic building
with more than 3960 s.m . carparks , industrial buildings , schools and
churches are exempted .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#120:1>
For very large buildings the storage chamber is designed as to
permit vehicular access to it for removal of refusal containers . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#121:1>
The storage chamber is located so as to have at least one
external wall .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#122:1>
Except for the access door and the hopper , there is no openings
inside the chamber which has no dimension less than 1.5m and ceiling height
not less than 2m .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#123:1>
Construction is usually of brickwork , blockwork or concrete .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#124:1>
Internal finishes for walls are glazed tiles , for ceiling it is
smooth cement rendering and for floor it is quarry tiles laid to fall towards
an outlet drain as well as with coved tile at the floor and wall junction .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#125:1>
A water supply point is installed inside . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#126:1>
The access door to the storage chamber is a close-fitting steel
door without any internal projections .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#127:1>
Size is not less than 1.8m high &times; 1.25m wide . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#128:1>
The refuse hopper is situated in upper floors in a place with
permanent ventilation to the open air .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#129:1>
Construction is of stainless or galvanized mild steel plate at least
3mm think and the mouth of which has a clear opening of size between 250 &times; 
150mm and 350 &times; 250mm . </p>

<p> 
<ICE-HK:W2A-035#130:1>
The refuse chute connecting the hoppers to the storage chamber
is a straight vertical tube offset not less than 60 degrees at the foot .

<ICE-HK:W2A-035#131:1>
The walls are of solid brick or concrete minimum 100mm thick with
internal diameter not less than 450mm and lined with glazed ware or other
impervious materials . </p> <&> Chinese translation </&>
