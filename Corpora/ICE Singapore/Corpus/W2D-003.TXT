<h> ADDITIONAL NOTES FOR REGISTRAR'S OFFICE STAFF IN CHARGE OF
EXAMINATION HALLS </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#102:3>
1)Before commencement of examinations ( a) Check the time-table to
ensure that the question papers you are bringing are meant for the
examinations in the hall.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#103:3>
( b) Hand over envelopes containing question papers to the Chief
Invigilator.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#104:3>
( c) Draw the attention of the Chief Invigilator to the proper use
of the attendance list, the colour of the answer books used for the
examination and the requirement to read out the Instructions to the
Candidates.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#105:3>
( d) Supervise clerical staff laying answer books on the tables.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#106:3>
Assist if necessary.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#107:3>
2) Admission of candidates into examination halls.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#108:3>
Remind Chief Invigilator to check and ensure that candidates do not
take books, files, bags, etc into the hall.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#109:3>
3) During the examination

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#110:3>
( a) You may return to office when all the candidates are present
and everything is in order.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#111:3>
If there are absentees at the end of the first half hour, take down
these names and remind the Chief Invigilator that these students should not
be admitted into the examination hall if they turn up later on.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#112:3>
( b) Before you leave the hall for office, check the toilets used by
candidates in the examination with the assistance of the clerical
staff/attendants to ensure that no books and notes are left in the cisterns
or under the wash basins in the toilets.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#113:3>
4) At the conclusion of examination

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#114:3>
( a) Be at the examination hall at least 20 minutes before the end
of the examination.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#115:3>
( b) Remind the Chief Invigilator to station invigilators at the
door to check and ensure that candidates do not take any answer scripts or
unused answer books out of the examination hall.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#116:3>
Assist the invigilators in this job.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#117:3>
( c) Help the invigilators to collect answer scripts if you have
been requested to.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#118:3>
( d) Ensure that the number of answer scripts and the signature of
the person who collected the scripts are filled in the Chief Invigilator's
report and acknowledgement slip.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#119:3>
( e) If the examiner for the paper under examination is present,
hand the scripts to him and ask him to sign on the acknowledgement slip.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#120:3>
( f) If the examiner is not present, count the scripts to ensure
that the number of scripts collected tallies with that in the Chief
Invigilator's Report.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#121:3>
Tie the scripts up with strings provided and bring them back to the
Examination Section.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#122:3>
( g) Collect the Chief Invigilator's Report and acknowledgement
slips, together with the list of candidates and return them to the
Examination Section.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#123:3>
5) Please inform the drivers of vans/minibuses to be stationed at
the Administration Building.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#124:3>
They should not go back to the Estate Office.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#125:3>
We may need to use the vans to run errands or fetch the University
Health Service doctor to the examination halls.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#126:3>
6) Return of keys - All keys are to be returned to the Examination
Section at the end of the day.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#127:3>
If your duty is for the morning session only, please return the keys
to the Examination Section together with the Chief Invigilator's Report and
acknowledgement slips after the morning session. </I>

<h> ADDITIONAL CONDITIONS OF ALLOCATION FOR RENTED PROPERTIES </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#130:4>
A) The staff member shall not:

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#131:4>
1) Take or permit to be made any alterations in the internal
construction or arrangements or in the external appearance or in the present
scheme of decoration of the premises without first obtaining the written
consent of the UDMC.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#132:4>
2) Do or permit to be done upon the said premises anything which in
the opinion of the UDMC which may at any time be or become a nuisance or
annoyance to or in any way interfere with the quiet and comfort of the
occupants of adjoining building or other adjoining occupiers of the
neighbourhood and not to use the same for any illegal or immoral purpose.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#133:4>
3) Keep or permit to be kept on the premises or any part thereof any
materials of a dangerous or explosive nature or the keeping of which may
contravene any local ordinance or regulations or by-laws.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#134:4>
4) Do or permit to be done anything whereby the policy or policies
of insurance ( if any) of the premises against damage by fire may become
void.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#135:4>
5) Affix or paint or otherwise exhibit on the exterior of the
premises or the windows thereof or any part thereof any nameplate, placard,
poster or advertisement or any flagstaff or any other thing whatsoever save
only the name of the tenant which shall be in such place or places and in
such manner and position as shall be approved by the UDMC.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#136:4>
6)Keep any wild animals or any reptile or bird in or upon the
premises or any part thereof or in any part thereof or in any compound of any
house comprised in the premises without obtaining the prior consent in
writing of UDMC.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#137:4>
B) The staff member shall:

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#138:4>
1)Keep the interior of the premises including the drains and
sanitary and water apparatus and the UDMC's fixtures therein and the doors
and windows thereof in good and tenantable repair and condition.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#139:4>
2)Use the premises as a private residential house for the personal
occupation only.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#140:4>
3)Permit the UDMC and it's agents, surveyors and workmen with all
necessary appliances to enter upon the said premises at all reasonable times
for the purpose either of viewing the condition thereof or of doing such
works and things in connection with any repairs, alternations or improvement
either of the said premises or of any part of any building in which the said
premises may be comprised or which they may adjoin.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#141:4>
4)At all times maintain the garden, grounds and drive of the
premises in good order and condition and not to alter the cultivation of
layout thereof without the written consent of the UDMC.

<h> PROHIBITION OF SMOKING IN THE LIBRARY </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#150:5>
1) According to the Smoking ( Prohibition in Certain Places) Act
1992, smoking inside the Library is not permitted ( See Appendix I) and any
person who is caught my be fined not more than
&dollar;1000 ( See Appendix II).

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#151:5>
2) You should therefore ask politely any user or staff member seen
smoking inside any part of the library building to cease smoking immediately
and to remind him of the penalty.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#152:5>
If the person refuses to comply, you should immediately report the
incident to your Head of Department.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#153:5>
3) In addition, if you are supervising any project involving
external workers, it is your responsibility to inform all the workers as soon
as they report to you that it is prohibited by law to smoke in the Library.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-003#154:5>
4) Your cooperation and help to enforce the no smoking law in the
library is very much appreciated.
