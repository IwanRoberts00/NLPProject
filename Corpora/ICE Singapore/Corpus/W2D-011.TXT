<h> TUG-BOAT EGGS </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#4:1>
( makes 8) - for kids

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#5:1>
<h> Ingredients </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#6:1>
4 hard-boiled eggs

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#7:1>
1 can tuna fish in brine ( drained)

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#8:1>
1 tablespoon natural yoghurt

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#9:1>
2 tablespoons mayonnaise

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#10:1>
Black pepper - ground

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#11:1>
2.5 cm ( 1 inch) piece of cucumber

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#12:1>
1 carrot

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#13:1>
8 bay leaves

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#14:1>
<h> Method </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#15:1>
Halve eggs length-wise, scoop out the yolks and sieve into the mixing
bowl.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#16:1>
Add the tuna fish to the yolks with the yoghurt, mayonnaise and
pepper to taste.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#17:1>
Mix with a fork until very smooth.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#18:1>
Cut a slice from the bottom of each egg so that it will stand evenly.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#19:1>
Put a spoonful of tuna mixture on each egg and shape into a mould
with a palette knife.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#20:1>
Cut the cucumber into eight 2.5 cm ( 1 inch) sticks and press
cucumber stick upright into each egg.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#21:1>
Do the same for the carrot and place it in front of the cucumber to
resemble funnels.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#22:1>
Press in the bay leaves to resemble sails.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#23:1>
Arrange on a bed of finely shredded lettuce to serve.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#71:4>
<h> BREAD PUDDING DELUXE </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#72:4>
or the elegant finale for five-day old bread

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#73:4>
This recipe is ideal for new cooks.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#74:4>
Any one of the ingredients can be increased or decreased or even
omitted, with impunity.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#75:4>
If the bread pudding emerges a little dry it services well, chilled
and with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top of it or warmed with lashings of
custard sauce over it.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#76:4>
If the pudding is soft-textured ( because of too much egg and milk)
baked custard lovers will relish it.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#77:4>
If it is not sweet enough, serve it with a teaspoon of apricot or
raspberry jam.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#78:4>
If you like puddings plain, omit the raisins, almond and ground
cinnamon; and leave out the wee drop of the spirit.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#79:4>
<h> Ingredients </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#80:4>
6 slices old white bread

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#81:4>
6 eggs

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#82:4>
3 ozs soft brown sugar

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#83:4>
0.5 pint UHT milk

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#84:4>
6 ozs raisin

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#85:4>
1 teaspoon brandy

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#86:4>
sprinkling of ground cinnamon

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#87:4>
3 ozs butter

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#88:4>
<h> Topping </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#89:4>
4 ozs slivered almonds

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#90:4>
2 ozs brown sugar

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#91:4>
sprinkling of ground cinnamon

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#92:4>
<h> Method </h>

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#93:4>
Break eggs into a bowl, add sugar and milk and beat mixture with an
egg whisk.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#94:4>
Add raisin, vanilla, cinnamon and brandy and leave to stand.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#95:4>
Butter a 9 x 9 baking dish and arrange 3 slices of buttered bread at
the bottom of the dish.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#96:4>
Spoon out the soaked raisins from the mixing bowl and place them
evenly over the bread.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#97:4>
Cover the raisins with 3 more slices of buttered bread.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#98:4>
Pour the rest of the mixture over the bread.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#99:4>
Spread topping evenly on top of the bread and dot the surface with
the remains of the butter.

<ICE-SIN:W2D-011#100:4>
Bake in a 150 degrees Celsius oven for 30-40 minutes or until the
top of the pudding is nicely brown.
